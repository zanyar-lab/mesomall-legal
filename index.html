Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mesopo Store ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and handling your data transparently. By using Mesopo Store, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the app's core shopping features:

  • Account Information. When you register or log in, we collect your name, email address, and a securely hashed password. This information is used to create and manage your account and to keep you signed in.
  • Order and Shipping Information. When you place an order, we collect the shipping details you provide, including your delivery address, city, phone number, and any order notes. This information is used solely to process and fulfill your orders.
  • Shopping Activity. We store information related to your use of the app's shopping features, such as the items in your cart, your wishlist, your order history, and any product reviews or ratings you submit.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, photos, or any information from your device beyond what you actively provide within the app.

Payment Information

Mesopo Store does not collect, process, or store real payment card details. Payment options presented in the app are for order-method selection only, and orders are handled on a cash-based or simulated basis. No real financial transactions are processed through the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, secure, and manage your account
  • Process, fulfill, and track your orders
  • Maintain your cart, wishlist, and order history
  • Display and manage the reviews and ratings you submit
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Maintain the security and proper functioning of the app

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

Third-Party Services

Mesopo Store communicates only with our own secure backend server to provide its features. We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or external tracking tools within the app.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on our own secured server infrastructure. Your login session is protected using industry-standard secure token storage on your device, and passwords are stored only in hashed form — never in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information associated with your account
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing, where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Mesopo Store is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
