Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app, tap "Register" on the welcome screen, and enter your name, email, and password to get started.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Password reset is coming soon. In the meantime, please contact us directly at the email above and we'll help you regain access to your account.

How do I track my order?

You can view your order history and status from your account profile inside the app.

How do I request my data or account be deleted?

Email us at zanyar@mesopo.net with your registered email address, and we'll process your deletion request promptly. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what data is removed.

Is my payment information secure?

MesoMall does not process or store real payment card details within the app. All checkout data is handled securely in line with our Privacy Policy.

The app isn't working properly — what should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or reinstalling it from the App Store / Google Play. If the issue continues, email us with a short description of the problem and, if possible, a screenshot.
